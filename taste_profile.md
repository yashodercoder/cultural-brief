# Taste Profile

## Draw me in
- Complex interiority, especially women's inner lives
- Atmospheric writing — mood as meaning
- High/low culture intersections (prestige TV meets literary theory)
- Cultural criticism that reveals human motivation or psychology
- Late-bloomer narratives, reinvention, creative ambition
- Sensory richness — food, place, texture woven into ideas
- Great food writing — criticism, memoir, culture, craft
- Poetry and contemplative work that rewards slowness
- Widely trending culture war pieces (if genuinely culturally significant)
- Obsessive fandom energy — deep dives on specific shows, games, genres (refine over time)
- Guilty pleasures taken seriously — smart writing about dumb things
- Witty, intelligent writing; humor that earns it
- Celebrity or reality TV only if the take is genuinely interesting and in-depth

## Skip
- Generic listicles, "best of," plot summaries
- Outrage pieces (unless trending widely)
- "What to binge/read before you die" — content-as-consumption framing
- Tech-bro productivity takes on reading or culture
- Celebrity gossip or reality TV recaps without a real angle
